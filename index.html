01
Observability Engineering

Setting up Prometheus exporters, building Grafana dashboards, configuring alert rules with precise thresholds — making invisible system behavior legible. Not just monitoring, but understanding what the numbers mean.

02
Production Debugging

Methodical incident response: read the logs, trace the request, identify the divergence. I'm comfortable in high-pressure situations and I don't guess — I follow the evidence to root cause.

03
Automation & Toil Reduction

Identifying repetitive operational work and writing scripts to eliminate it. Python, Bash, and a bias toward simplicity — if a human is doing it more than twice, it should probably be automated.

04
System Reliability Design

Thinking about what happens when services fail, how systems degrade gracefully, and what retry/backoff strategies look like in practice — building with failure in mind from the start.
